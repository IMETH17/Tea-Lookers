Black Tea

The traditional English cup of tea is a black tea, but actually it’s not the most ancient form of the drink rather a more recent development. It differs from green and other tea types in the way it is made, having gone through a process of full oxidation. This gives it a more malty or rich and sweet flavour that some, particularly the British, love to enjoy with added milk.
