Ceylon Tea Dyed Branded Face Mask Dye is a By-Product of Ice Tea Manufacturing

Having the objective of increasing the awareness level on Ceylon Tea as a global brand, Sri Lanka Tea Board has started distributing Ceylon Tea branded face mask among the business community worldwide on complimentary basis. With the collaboration of the Ministry of Foreign Relations, arrangements have been made by Sri Lanka Tea Board to dispatch Ceylon Tea Branded face masks to over seventy five (75) Sri Lankan missions around the world. The specialty of Ceylon Tea branded face mask is that it has been dyed with the use of Ceylon Tea Dye which is a by-product of Ice Tea manufacturing.
